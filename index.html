What is MEDcipher?

MEDcipher is a smart clinical decision support system (CDSS) designed to enable a rapid assessment of risk and can guide assessment, diagnosis, and intervention at the point of patient contact.

Algorithms and diagnosis

Current access to modern healthcare means that many diseases are now treatable. However, patients cannot access solutions without accurate diagnosis.

​The main gateway to healthcare is primary care and General Practitioners. Many conditions require a level of specialism of experience which is unevenly distributed at the primary care level.

Optimising the diagnostic process by integrating what we already have; patient records and GP consultation, leads to1-4​

  • faster diagnosis and thus treatment​
  • improved patient experience​
  • improved outcomes​
  • cost efficiency. ​

This tool is a clinical decision support system (CDSS) designed to accelerate disease detection by supporting primary care physicians to provide specialised assessment in a general health setting.

UK Rare Diseases Framework

Priority one in the UK Rare Diseases Framework is obtaining the right diagnosis (through screening programmes which provide early-stage testing including genetic tests) faster, in order to access the best symptomatic and therapeutic strategies.1
